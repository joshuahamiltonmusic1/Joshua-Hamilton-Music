Joshua Hamilton

Composer & Sound Designer

Joshua Hamilton is an accomplished composer, producer, and multi-instrumentalist with over 13 years of musical experience. Currently in his third year of college, Joshua is studying piano performance, sound design, music technology, and narrative composition in MTU.

Selected Credits

  • "The Young Offenders" (Season 5) — Music & Score Production

Industry Profile

Bridging the gap between academic mastery and high-tier industry production, Joshua has spent seven years cultivating a signature sonic footprint as a producer.

Television & Cinema

His versatility as a dramatic storyteller was recently demonstrated on the score for the hit television series "The Young Offenders," where he worked closely alongside acclaimed composer Ray Harmon.

Sonic Philosophy

Whether crafting intimate, piano-driven themes or engineering immersive, multi-layered electronic soundscapes, Joshua delivers evocative, custom-tailored music that elevates visual narratives and shapes unforgettable cinematic experiences.
